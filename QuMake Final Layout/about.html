About Us:
Hello, and thank you for visiting The QuMaker. Our aim is to teach children or toddlers who are still unable to attend school. All it requires is a phone and a connection to the internet, which, by today’s standards, is commonplace.The website is easy to use because it consists of just flashcards and multiple-choice questions in the test. The website is divided into categories for colors, math, sounds, and shapes. The website aims to be as coherent as possible so that a child may understand it.
The test features a progress bar to assess your understanding of what you’ve learned so far. If the progress bar from your previous test does not reach the standard passing score of 75% or above, it will automatically reset, forcing you to repeat the test. This puts the learner under pressure to get a good score to continue. But don't worry; if the student gets it incorrect, the correct answer and an explanation will be provided at the end of the test so that the student may evaluate his or her mistakes. If the students fail the test, a motivating message will be displayed at the end of the test.
